Genovese Pesto Pasta

Fresh basil, garlic, olive oil and love â€” a classic Italian favorite.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Blend basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan, and olive oil until smooth.
  3. Toss the pesto with the cooked pasta and serve with extra Parmesan.
